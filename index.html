About Me

Hi my name is Harry Thrall, I am a front-end web developer, specialising in HTML, CSS and Javascript.

I graduated from the University of York in 2018 with a Master's Degree in Information Technology. With a strong knowledge of Java behind me I pushed myself to learn new skills and expand my programming knowledge. It was here my passion for web development began. I quickly realised how much I enjoyed taking my projects from a rough sketch in my notepad, to a fully fledged working website. For my larger projects such as the wedding website I organise my requirements using Trello which allows me to see what functionality I haave completed and what I still have to add.

I still have a lot to learn on this journey but I think with programming you never stop learning, be it new frameworks, new techniques or even new languages altogether but the ever changing nature is what makes this field so exciting.
